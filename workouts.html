Push-ups

The push-ups code is a popular exercise that targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps muscles. It involves pushing your body up from the ground using your arms.

To perform the push-ups code, you can follow these steps:

  1. Start in a plank position with your hands directly under your shoulders and your feet together or slightly apart.
  2. Lower your body towards the ground until your chest nearly touches the floor.
  3. Push your body back up to the starting position.
  4. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Tips

  • Keep your body in a straight line from your head to your heels.
  • Avoid arching your back or sagging your hips.
  • Focus on contracting your chest, shoulder, and triceps muscles.
  • If you're a beginner, you can start by doing push-ups on your knees.

Chin-Ups Guide

Chin-ups are a challenging exercise that target the biceps, lats, and forearms.

To perform a chin-up:

  1. Hang from a pull-up bar with an overhand grip, hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
  2. Pull your body up until your chin clears the bar.
  3. Pause at the top and then slowly lower yourself back down.

Tips

  • Focus on engaging your back muscles.
  • Avoid swinging your legs or body.
  • If you're struggling, try using a resistance band to assist you.
